# Power and Electrical Design

## Gateway Power

- operates at 5V  
- Raspberry Pi 4 requires approximately 3A supply  
- Raspberry Pi 5 requires up to 5A with peripherals  
- stable power supply required for continuous operation  

---

## Sensor Node Power

- operates on 3.3V logic  
- powered via USB or battery  
- wireless transmission causes short power spikes  
- power design must account for peak current  

---

## Design Considerations

- avoid unstable or low quality power supplies  
- ensure sufficient current for all connected devices  
- consider battery life for wireless nodes  
- use efficient transmission intervals to reduce power usage
